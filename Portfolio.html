These are some of the current exciting projects I'm working on!

Rubinov Lab (2024-Present)

I am currently an undergraduate at the Rubinov Lab at Vanderbilt, where I am currently working on projects relating to ​​integrative models of large-scale neural data. Right now, I am tasked with converting certain tools requiring parallelism from Spark to Dask.

HIVE (2022-Present)

Hive is a project that I began developing senior year of high school. The purpose of hive is to build a sensor suite in the form of a base for bee hives. The purpose of the sensors is to monitor factors vital to bee health, which has become a prevailing issue due to climate change. The vision is to place these hives in remote areas, connect all of them together and get overall trends in bee health around the country.

PAST PROJECTS.

NASA Planetary Environments Laboratory (2022-2023)

I worked with the NASA Planetary Environments Laboratory to write code that would process raw data from the Gas Chromatography Mass Spectrometer (GCMS) that analysed soil samples from Mars. I also worked on artificial data generation to test certain equipment.

Brain Aging and Dementia Lab @ HMS/MGH (2021-2022)

I was a visitng student at the Brain Aging and Dementia Lab at Harvard Medical School/Massachusetts General Hospital. I attended weekly lab meetings, shadowed researchers and was given reading assignments to learn more about the issues the lab was working on.

Gottlieb Laboratory @ Columbia (2020-2021)

I was a student researcher at the Gottlieb Lab located at Columbia University, where I was mentored by two Ph.D. students and helped out with ongoing Neuroscience research, specifically in the field of attention and decision-making. I specifically worked on creating an experimental model with python based on the research done by one of the Ph.D. students.

United Nations, Nairobi (2022,2023)

I worked with the communications department, where I was assigned with tasks like organizing press conferences, writing press releases and summary articles for the main UN webpage.

In 2023, I worked at the 2nd session of the United Nations Habitat Assembly, where I was tasked with running the social media accounts of the Executive Director and the Deputy Executive Dircetor of UN-Habitat.
